Generation 1 Starter Pokemon

Pikachu is an electric-type pokemon introduced in Generation 1. It evolves from Pichu when leveled up with high friendship and evolves into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone.

Bulbasaur is a dual type Grass/Poison Pokemon introduced in Generation 1. It evolves into Ivysaur at level 16, which evolves into Venasaur at level 32.

Charmander is a Fire-Type Pokemon introduced in Generation 1. It evolves into Charmeleon at level 16, which evolves into Charizard starting at level 36.

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokemon introduced in Generation 1. It evolves into Wartortle starting at level 16, which evolves into Blastoise starting at level 36.
